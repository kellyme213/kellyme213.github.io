Ray Tracer

I am fascinated by the field of computer graphics. By following an online tutorial, I programmed my own ray tracer. The tutorial only had rendering support for spheres, but I extended my ray tracer to include rendering support for cones, cylinders, planes, and polygons. Additionally, I implemented the functionality to add multiple lights to a scene, allowing for complex renders.
