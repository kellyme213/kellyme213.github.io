OXIO

OXIO is a puzzle game currently in development for iOS and macOS set for release in 2018. In OXIO, the player moves the center of mass of a group of blocks to the goal. This is accomplished by changing the mass of the blocks and moving the blocks around the screen.

To speed up game development, I programmed a real-time level editor in the macOS version of the game.

A demo version of OXIO earned me a ticket to Apple's 2017 Worldwide Developers Conference.
