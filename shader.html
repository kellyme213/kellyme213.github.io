Fun With Shaders

Some shaders I've written for various projects.

Toon/Cell Shader

An OpenGL Fragment shader. The shader samples a normal map, and if the normal transformed into screen space dotted with the z axis is close to 0, the shader draws a black line.

Procedural Wood Shader

A Metal Fragment shader.

Procedural Brick Shader

A Metal Fragment shader.

Mandelbrot Shader

A Metal Fragment Shader takes the 3d position of the pixel, turns it into a complex number, and determines if the number lies in the Mandelbrot set. I also pass in a time parameter that modifies the complex numbers to animate the surface of the shader.

Alarm Shader

A Unity Surface Shader for my game, Duality. The shader causes the red on the object to pulse.
