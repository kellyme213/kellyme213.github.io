Connect the Shapes!

Connect the Shapes! is a cross between a match-three game and connect the dots. Shapes are cleared from the board when they are linked together with shapes of the same type.

A demo version of Connect the Shapes! earned me a ticket to Apple's 2016 Worldwide Developers Conference.
